Abstract

For example, in 2022, the failure rate was 22%, and by 2025, that rate had reached almost 40%.

Based on this, a prototype called LegalRadar was developed, initially focused on traffic tickets and violations. It is designed to facilitate learning through dynamic activities with immediate feedback and is divided into categories. The proposal aims to offer an accessible alternative that reinforces road safety knowledge and motivates drivers.
